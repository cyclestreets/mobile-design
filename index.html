Mapping

Mapping data courtesy of OpenStreetMap. Without OSM mappers on the ground collecting great data, the routing would not be possible. OpenStreetMap data can be used freely under the terms of the Open Data Commons Open Database License (ODbL).

OpenStreetMap is the Wiki of maps. YOU can help improve the routing and location data, by going to www.openstreetmap.org and contributing your local knowledge!

Routing by CycleStreets uses a custom-written routing engine.

Feedback on planned routes is very welcome, in this app or at www.cyclestreets.net/feedback, and helps us improve the algorithms and data.

Map tiles thanks to OpenStreetMap, and OpenCycleMap.
